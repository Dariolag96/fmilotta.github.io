Biography

Filippo Luigi Maria Milotta is a member of the Image Processing Laboratory (IPLab), within the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science of the University of Catania. He is currently a PhD candidate in a doctorate fellowship granted by TIM Telecom Italia in “Multi-device Media Analysis and Summarization for High Bandwidth Connected Environments”.
